Outfit Scoring

105-point system: Color Harmony (30), Skin Flattery (20), Body Shape (20), Weather (20), Occasion (10), Trending (5).

Color Theory

HSL color wheel: Complementary (180°), Analogous (±30°), Monochromatic (same hue). Calculates harmony scores.

Constraints

Outfit rules: top+bottom OR dress (not both), outerwear when cold, formal occasion requirements.
